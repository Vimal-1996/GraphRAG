**Financial Times - July 18, 2024**

TechVision Inc. announced its expansion into the European market with the opening of a new office in London. The office will serve as the company's European headquarters and will house 100 employees by the end of 2025.

Emma Wilson, formerly of DeepMind, has been appointed as Managing Director of European Operations. Wilson will report directly to CRO Michael Zhang.

The company also secured regulatory approval from the European Medicines Agency (EMA) for MediScan AI, allowing it to be deployed in hospitals across the European Union.
